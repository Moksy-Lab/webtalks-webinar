From practical sessions to industry-recognised certification, WebTalks equips you with real skills, expert insights, and flexible learning. Master SEO, ads, content, and analytics—then prove it. Learn, apply, and grow—only with WebTalks.

4-Module Digital Marketing Sprint

Mod-1

Foundations & Strategy

The course begins by establishing a strong foundation in digital marketing.You'll learn how to define clear marketing objectives, identify your target audience, and build customer journeys using funnels. This week focuses on strategic thinking, brand positioning, and competitor research, preparing to plan campaigns with purpose and clarity.

Mod-2

SEO & Content Marketing

In the second week,dive into search engine optimisation and content creation.Explore keyword research, on-page SEO techniques, and content structuring for blogs, websites, and social media. Through hands-on tasks,Learn how to craft content that ranks well and drives engagement, using tools like SEMrush and Google Search Console.

Mod-3

Paid Advertising & Campaign Setup

This week focuses on launching real ad campaigns across platforms like Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram). You’ll set campaign objectives, define ad targeting, create ad creatives, and manage budgets. Practical sessions guide you through split testing and tracking, helping you understand performance metrics and how to optimise results in real time.

Mod-4

Analytics, Email Marketing & Reporting

The final week covers performance analysis and retention strategies. You’ll use tools like Google Analytics and Mailchimp to interpret campaign data, set up email automation flows, and present insights in client-ready formats. By the end, you’ll know how to scale campaigns, retain users, and communicate marketing impact clearly.
